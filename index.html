What we do

SVF Medical Corporation’s core business is the research, innovate, prototype, manufacture and distribute products that SVF Medical Corporation gain patents and rights to, including the intended flagship product, currently a concept based on the innovative combination of two existing technologies. Douglas Anchell has consulted on the technological feasibility and has got positive feedback on the possibility.

SVF Medical Corporation shall be on a lookout for interesting product innovations and inventions globally and develop new products to expand the range of medical devices that SVF may introduce to the market in the future.

Flagship SVF

Disruptive Innovation

SVF is not designed to compete with any existing devices available in the market.

Proven Technology

The conventional syringe meets vein illuminating near-infrared light and laser technology. Vein illuminating light is an existing technology that is used in high-end devices

Prototyping

The research and development process will include prototype fabrication. This will include 3D design, 3D modelling, 3D printing and technical drawing/model. The prototype will be tested, adjusted and further refined.

International Market access

After the stringent research and development process, the selected components shall be sourced and manufactured by an ISO 13485 certified manufacturer.

This will ensure instantaneous access to more market worldwide, meets regulatory requirements that apply to medical devices, components, contract services and distribution of medical devices.

Highly attractive to the private healthcare sector, e.g. a client seeking cosmetic surgery who may choose to use SVF syringes to avoid pain in the Phlebotomy process.”

Due to the growing population and an increase of life expectancy, the consumption of medical devices and disposables are increasing in the healthcare industry.
